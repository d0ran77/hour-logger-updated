Privacy & Terms

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Google API Disclosure

Practisy is a "Local-First" productivity tool. We prioritize your privacy and clinical confidentiality by ensuring your data never leaves your device.

Data Accessed (Google User Data)

Practisy requests read-only access to your Google Calendar (.../auth/calendar.readonly). Specifically, our Sync Engine accesses:

  • Event Titles & Summaries: To identify sessions based on your custom keywords.
  • Start & End Times: To automatically calculate clinical contact hours.
  • Event Color IDs: To categorize sessions (e.g., Client vs. Supervision) if color-matching is enabled.

Data Usage & Purpose

The accessed data is used solely to automate your clinical log. The application processes these events locally in your browser to calculate professional compliance ratios and progress toward clinical goals. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, stored in a central database, or shared with third parties.

Limited Use Policy

Practisy's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Terms of Service

  • Data Responsibility: Practisy is entirely local. You are solely responsible for maintaining manual backups using the built-in .json export tool.
  • Ethics: You remain responsible for compliance with your respective governing bodies (e.g., BACP, UKCP).
  • Anonymity: We recommend using initials or pseudonyms in your calendar to maintain absolute client anonymity.
